Jennifer

Owner

Jennifer McCollough is the owner and an esthetician at LC Spa. In 1994, Jennifer moved to Europe from China and took a special interest in European skincare. When Jennifer moved to America, she brought a wealth of skincare knowledge and techniques. Jennifer received her CA esthetician’s license from San Diego’s top beauty school, the Bellus Academy. She specializes in Anti-Aging, TCA Peel, Jessner Peel, Vita C-oxy, Micro-Current face lifting, fat burning, RF treatment, and removal of skin growths.
