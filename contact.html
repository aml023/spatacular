FAQs

To ensure you receive all the spa treatments you want, when you want, not only is advance booking suggested, it is highly recommended. Guests may book up to one month in advance of their arrival. Some treatments may not be available every day.
The aestheticians at LC spa are trained to customize each of our facial treatments for your particular skin through skin analysis. This may mean that you do not receive the facial you booked but one rather more suited to your skin type.
Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled appointment or a 50% charge will be applied.
Young adults between the ages of 12–17 may freely enjoy the following without the supervision of a parent or guardian: Salon services such as make-up, pedicures, manicures and hair services. Young adults must be supervised by parent or guardian while participating in Spa Services including massage and body treatments, facials or any one-on-one services.
Please notify a spa professional before scheduling your appointment and they will suggest a plan that’s right for you
We carry a full array of professional spa products so you can duplicate the benefits at home. We also ship direct to you should you want to reorder upon returning home.
Shaving is recommended but please be sure to do so at least two hours prior to your scheduled appointment.
Arriving late may limit the time for your treatment. Depending on the length of your service, a late arrival may be classified as a ‘no-show.’
For beauty salon services, we recommend check-in at least 5 minutes prior to your service. For massage treatments, check in with the reception desk at least 10 minutes prior to your appointment. This will allow you enough time to register, change into your robe and relax for a few minutes before your spa treatment begins.
